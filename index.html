Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at Computer Science Department, Graduate Center, City University of New York (CUNY). [Curriculum Vitae]

Now I work with Prof. Liang Huang in Algorithms for Computational Linguistics (ACL) Group. My research is focused on algorithms and theory in Natural Language Processing (NLP), especially in structured prediction problems including parsing and machine translation. I am also very interested in Machine Learning like online learning and large margin methods.

Prior to CUNY, I received my Bachelor of Engineering (B.Eng.) degree from Computer Science Department, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2010.
